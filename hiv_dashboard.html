1 · Close the coverage gaps

Channel treatment funding to low-ART, high-burden countries — Pakistan, Indonesia, Madagascar, Congo, Sudan. The largest available mortality win.

2 · Re-energise prevention

With new infections plateauing, renew PrEP, testing and education — focused on Africa and rising-infection settings like the Philippines.

3 · Fund the growing care pool

Lock in sustainable, long-term financing for lifelong ART so hard-won mortality gains are never reversed.

4 · Target retention hotspots

In Lesotho, Ethiopia, Malawi and Zimbabwe, prioritise early diagnosis, adherence and viral suppression over prevention alone.

5 · Modernise data reporting

Move the remaining five-yearly indicators to annual reporting everywhere for timelier monitoring and faster course-correction.
